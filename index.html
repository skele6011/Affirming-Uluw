The Qur'an

The Qur'an is the holy book of Islam, believed by Muslims to be a revelation from God. It serves as a guide for all aspects of life.

The Sunnah/The Sahaba

The Sunnah refers to the teachings and practices of the Prophet Muhammad, while the Sahaba are his companions who played a vital role in spreading his message.

The Salaf/Scholars

The Salaf refers to the early generations of Muslims, particularly the first three generations, who are revered for their piety and understanding of Islam.
